SUPPORTING CHILDREN

In the morning, everyone gathered at a community center in a remote area, where there was a group of lovely children living in difficult circumstances. Warmth and joy spread as people began to create new relationships and interact with the children. There were fun games, learning sessions and experience sharing between volunteers and children.

During noon, a party was prepared, with many delicious dishes and cakes. This is the time when people gather to chat and enjoy delicious food together. A small gift was also given to each child, bringing smiles and indescribable happiness.

In the afternoon, the event continued with art and sports activities. Children discover and develop their talents in these fields under the guidance of experienced experts and volunteers. Finally, the event ended with a musical performance performed by the children themselves, to show off their talent and confidence.

This event to support children in remote areas not only brings joy and hope to the children but also awakens compassion and solidarity in all of us. It is a clear testament to the power of community solidarity and its ability to change the lives of those in need.
